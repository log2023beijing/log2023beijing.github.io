LoG Meetup Beijing, China 2023
Nov 18, 2023
Beijing

What is this about?

In conjuction with the 2023 Learning on Graphs (LoG) Conference, we are organizing a local meetup in the China Beijing. The meetup will take place on the Saturday, November 18th. It will be hosted by AITIME. The meetup will feature keynote talks, a panel discussion, and a social event. This meetup aims to bring together the local Graph ML community, fostering discussions and collaborations while encouraging more students to get involved.

Key information

  • 1. What? A Graph ML Meetup for the LoG Conference 2023.

  • 2. Where? TBD.

  • 3. When? 18th of November 2023, all day.

  • 4. Objective: Connect the local graph learning community in the region.

  • 5. Who is invited? Everyone.

  • 6. Cost? free

  • Note: We are majorly supported by AITIME, the Learning-on-Graphs conference 2023
